Game Design

Game design is something that's interested me for a long time. I grew up playing games in my spare time, it was one of my favorite pastimes... My best friend and I would spend a ridiculous amount of time playing Halo, but not for the main draws like the campaign or the versus multiplayer. We would play it for the forge mode. A mode where you could make your own maps and gamemodes. We hardly ever played much of the maps we made, we would just create them and talk about how they would played. That was good enough for us. However, all that time playing forge piqued my interest. What makes a game good? Better yet, how do you even begin to make one?

Historical Figures

This is more of a recent interest of mine that was sparked by my grandpa giving me a bunch of biographies that he had read. I've never been much of an avid reader, but I figured I would try it out. So I started reading the biography of the Wright brothers. I remembered learning a tiny bit about them in school and figured it might be interesting. I think it gave interesting insight into the times as well. There was so much interest in flight at the time, but so many failed attempts led people to believe nothing would come of it.
