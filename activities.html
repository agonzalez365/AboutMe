Trails

Going out in nature can be a nice outing. There's plenty of good places to go on walks in Washington. My go-to spot is the Coal Creek Natural Area. It's close, it has a creek and waterfalls, and it has some history with the early coal mining industry with some parts of the trail once being where tracks were used haul coal out.
